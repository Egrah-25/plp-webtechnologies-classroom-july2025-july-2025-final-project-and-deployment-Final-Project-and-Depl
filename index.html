About Me

Capturing the Essence of Africa

My name is Egrah Savai, and I'm a professional photographer specializing in documenting the diverse cultures, landscapes, and people of Africa.

With over 5 years of experience, I've traveled across 15 African countries to capture the true spirit and beauty of this magnificent continent.

My work has been featured in National Geographic, BBC Travel, and several international exhibitions. I believe in ethical photography that respects and celebrates the subjects while telling authentic stories.

When I'm not behind the camera, I teach photography workshops across Africa and serve as a board member for the African Photographers Association.
